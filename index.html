About Me

Hello, my name is Rajiv Bedse.

Chief Executive Officer

Rajiv is a results-driven Immigrant Job-Search Mentor, Certified Resume Writer, and a Public Speaker helping immigrant professionals to launch their new career in Australia fast track.

After spending almost half of his life in India Rajiv migrated to Australia in late 90's with his wife and two teen-aged children, no job, and very little money.

Coming from Civil Engineering background Rajiv had to make a big career change. But within just a few years he became the top BDM out of 35 others, working for Telstra.

Helping people to thrive in life is Rajiv's passion. Empathy and heart-to-heart connection is crucial for immigrant career coaching, and you will surely notice it in Rajiv.

Rajiv has been in the workforce since the last over 40 years.
